

      006  Upstairs and downstairs  楼上和楼下
      
      What the writer want to express
          The writer's original version of Chinese
      
      甲：王夫人在哪儿？
      乙：她在楼上。
      甲：景先生在哪儿？
      乙：他在楼下。
      甲：洪小姐做什么？
      乙：她在上楼去做一些复印件。
      甲：告诉她马上下楼来。
      
      
      My correction
      
      甲：王夫人在哪儿？
      乙：她在楼上。
      甲：景先生在哪儿？
      乙：他在楼下。
      甲：洪小姐在做什么？
      乙：她在上楼弄复印件。
      甲：告诉她马上下楼来。
      
      The writer's native language version
      
      A：Where is Mrs. Wang?
      B：She’s upstairs. 
      A：Where is Mr. Jing? 
      B：He’s downstairs. 
      A：What is Miss Hong doing? 
      B：She’s going upstairs to make some photocopies. 
      A：Tell her to come downstairs immediately.
      
      Comparison of the two languages  中英文对照
      
      甲：王夫人在哪儿？
      Where's Mrs Wang?
      
      乙：她在楼上。
      She's upstairs.
      
      甲：景先生在哪儿？
      Where's Mr Jing?
      
      乙：他在楼下。
      He's downstairs.
      
      甲：洪小姐做什么？
      What is Miss Hong doing?
      
      乙：她上楼去弄些复印件。
      She's upstairs to make some photocopies.
      
      甲：告诉她马上下楼来。
      Tell her come downstairs immediately.
      
      笔记                                                    Notes
      她在楼上                                                She's upstairs.
      
      我在楼下                                                I'm downstairs.
      
      他在复印文件                                            He's making photocopies.
      
      马上下楼来                                              Come downstairs immediately.


    008  Ticket office  售票亭
    
    What the writer want to express
    The writer's original version of Chinese
    
    先生：您好，我想出去找什么吃的东西，然后回来。
    服务员：您要离开公园吗？
    先生：对，找什么吃的东西，马上再进公园。
    服务员：没问题。
    先生：需要再买另一张票吗？
    服务员：不需要，门票整天都好。
    先生：太棒了！谢谢。
    服务员：没事儿。
    
    My correction
    
    游客：您好，我想出去吃点儿东西，然后回来。
    服务员：您要离开公园吗？
    游客：对，吃点儿东西，马上就回来。
    服务员：没问题。
    游客：需要再买一张票吗？
    服务员：不需要，门票整天都能用(有效)。
    游客：太棒了！谢谢。
    服务员：没事儿。
    
    The writer's native language version
    
    Man:          Hello, I'd like to go out of the park to find something to eat and then come back in.
    Attendant:    Do you want to leave the park?
    Man:          Yes. To find something to eat and come right back.
    Attendant:    No problem.
    Man:          Will I need to buy another ticket?
    Attendant:    No, the ticket is good all day.
    Man:          Great! Thank you!
    Attendant:    No problem.
    
    Comparison of the two languages  中英文对照
    
    游客：您好，我想吃点儿东西，然后回来。
    Man:  Hello, I'd like to go out of the park to find something to eat and then come back.
    服务员：您要离开公园吗？
    Attendant:  Do you want to leave the park?
    游客：对，吃点儿东西，马上就回来。
    Man:  Yes. To find something to eat and come right back.
    服务员：没问题。
    Attendant:  No problem.
    游客：需要再买一张票吗？
    Man:  Will I need to buy another ticket?
    服务员：不需要，门票整天都能用(有效)。
    Attendant:  No, the ticket is good all day.
    游客：太棒了！谢谢。
    Man: Great! Thank you!
    服务员：没事儿。
    Attendant:  No problem.
    
    笔记                                                                                              Notes
        
    1  I'd like to 听起来比 I want 更礼貌些                                                           I'd(I would) like to & I want                                                                  
    I'd like to 更理智些, 而I want 更主观些                                                           "I would like to" sounds more polite and it's more like an intention while                        
    请求陌生人或不熟悉的人要说I'd like to 而不是 I want                                               "I want" is more like a desire.                                                                   
    
    2  出去吃点东西                                                                                   go out (of somewhere) to find something to eat.
    
    3  马上回来                                                                                       come right back
    
    4  good还有有效的意思. 票整天都有效                                                               The ticket is good all day.
